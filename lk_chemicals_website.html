Our Products

Complete range of water treatment chemicals — RO, Boiler, Cooling Tower, Descaling and more.

RO Treatment
RO Antiscalant

Prevents mineral scale on RO membranes. Available for standard, high silica and low silica water conditions.

RO Treatment
RO Membrane Cleaner (Low pH)

Removes inorganic scales — calcium carbonate and metal oxides — from fouled RO membranes.

RO Treatment
RO Membrane Cleaner (High pH)

Removes organic fouling, biological deposits and silt from reverse osmosis membranes.

RO Treatment
RO pH Booster / Mineral Chemicals

Adjusts permeate pH and re-mineralises purified water for potable and process applications.

Boiler
Boiler Antiscalant

Inhibits scale in boiler tubes and heat exchangers, reducing maintenance downtime.

Boiler
Boiler Oxygen Scavenger

Removes dissolved oxygen from boiler feed water to prevent pitting corrosion.

Boiler
Boiler pH Booster

Maintains optimal alkalinity in boiler water to protect metal surfaces from corrosion.

Cooling Tower
Cooling Tower Biocide

Controls bacteria, algae and Legionella in open recirculating cooling water systems.

Cooling Tower
Cooling Tower Antiscalant

Prevents calcium carbonate, sulphate and silica scaling in cooling water circuits.

Cooling Tower
Cooling Tower Anti-Corrosion

Descaling
Industrial Descaling Chemical

Descaling
Scale Master Chemical
